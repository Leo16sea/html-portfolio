Leo's Top 3 Movies

This List shows my top 3 movies

The Lion King

This is a great movie about growing up and holding yourself accountatble.

The Boy and The Heron

This is a wonderful movie that shows a young boy who gets to meet his mother after her unfortunate passing. It shows the love of a mother and son as well as how the mother will still go on to bring her son into being despite knowing that she herself has a tragic fate that will happen a few years after she brings her son into the world

Black Panther

Another movie that is similar to the The Lion King but details a man who has to take the place of his father on the throne after his passing. He must also take up the mantle of Black Panter in order to serve and protect his country and the world at large alongside the super hero group known as the Avengers.
